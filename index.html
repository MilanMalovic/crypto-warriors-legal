Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Choose a cryptocurrency, predict if the price will go UP or DOWN, and compete against friends, users, or virtual opponents. The game is ready in 10 seconds and correct predictions win!

How do I purchase coins?

Go to the Store section in the app and select a coin package.

How do I add friends?

Search for friends by username in the Friends section.

How does XP work?

Earn XP by winning games, completing daily challenges, and maintaining win streaks.
