Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Crypto Warriors ("we", "our", "us") operates the Crypto Warriors mobile game ("Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information – When signing up using Firebase Authentication (email/password or Google Login), we may collect your email address and basic profile information provided by Google.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information – Anonymized device identifiers, operating system, app version.
  • Gameplay Data – In-game actions, match results, balances of virtual play money coins.
  • Server Communication – Game results and state synchronization sent to our Node.js server.

1.3 Ad-Related Information

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads, including rewarded ads. AdMob may automatically collect device identifiers for advertising, such as:

  • IP address
  • Advertising ID (AAID/IDFA)
  • App interactions with ads

AdMob processes this data under Google's own Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Play Money Disclaimer

The game does NOT use real money.

All currency inside Crypto Warriors (coins, rewards, winnings) is strictly virtual play money with no cash value. You cannot deposit, withdraw, trade, or exchange anything for real currency.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account via Firebase Authentication
  • To synchronize game progress with our Node.js server
  • To operate gameplay features, matchmaking, and virtual economy
  • To show ads (AdMob) and offer rewarded ads
  • To improve app performance and security

4. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal data.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Firebase (authentication and secure access control)
  • Google AdMob (ads and rewarded ads)
  • Our Node.js game server (gameplay processing)

All third-party partners comply with GDPR and Google Play policies.

5. Data Security

We use Firebase-provided security features, encrypted communication (HTTPS), and server-side validation to protect your data. No method of transmission is 100% secure, but we follow industry standards.

6. Children's Privacy

Crypto Warriors is not intended for users under the age required by Google Play (13+ or regional equivalent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Account deletion
  • Access to your saved data
  • Correction of any inaccurate data

To request removal or changes, contact us at: crypto.warriors@yahoo.com

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes are posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
